========================================================================
  Selective-Veto Saturation Diagnostic
========================================================================

Root cause: when 100% of proposals are resource-modifying,
SelectiveVeto vetoes everything, yielding executor_count = 0.

This occurs when the environment does not produce the observation
types needed for advisory proposals (e.g., CPU pressure for
WorkloadClassifyRecommend).

Proposal-type breakdowns above show the mix per scenario.
If a real system shows 100% resource-modifying proposals,
check:
  1. Does /proc/pressure/cpu exist and return valid data?
  2. Are non-resource-modifying agents (e.g., ObserveOnly) registered?
  3. Is the workload generating the right observation types?
